About Us

Longwood Innovations, LLC was founded in 2018 by researchers from Harvard, MIT, Cornell, and several biotech companies in the Boston/Cambridge area. With deep roots in both academia and industry, we are well positioned to serve the unmet scientific needs of biotech and pharma clients.

We provide in-depth analysis of cutting-edge scientific approaches with input from field-specific experts, and coordination and interpretation with contract research organizations (CROs) to ensure they meet the standards required to move your research forward.
